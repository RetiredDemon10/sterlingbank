About Sterling Trust Bank

Building trust and financial prosperity in our communities since 1975.

Our Story

Founded in 1975 by a group of local business leaders, Sterling Trust Bank began with a simple mission: to provide exceptional banking services that help individuals and businesses in our community achieve their financial goals.

Over the decades, we've grown from a single branch to a regional banking powerhouse, but we've never forgotten our roots. Every decision we make is guided by our commitment to our customers and communities.

Today, Sterling Trust Bank stands as a testament to what's possible when you combine traditional banking values with modern innovation and unwavering dedication to customer service.

Security Tips for Safe Banking

Follow these best practices to keep your accounts secure.

Password Security

  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Never share login credentials
  • Update passwords regularly

Safe Browsing

  • Always log in from our official website
  • Look for the padlock icon in your browser
  • Never click links in suspicious emails
  • Log out completely when finished

Monitor Your Accounts

  • Check accounts regularly
  • Set up account alerts
  • Report suspicious activity immediately
  • Review monthly statements carefully
